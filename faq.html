Frequently Asked Questions

What is Remote Monitoring and Management (RMM)?

RMM is a way to watch over and manage your computer systems from afar, helping businesses keep everything running smoothly.

How does RMM save money compared to traditional Remote Support services?

RMM stops work from stopping, keeps everything up-to-date, and fixes little problems before they get big, saving businesses money.

Is remote monitoring safe?

Our RMM system uses strong security to make sure your information stays safe, even when managed from afar.

Can RMM stop hackers?

While RMM helps a lot with security, it's part of a bigger plan to keep hackers out. It makes it much harder for them but isn't a complete solution on its own.

How does RMM improve productivity?

By proactively addressing issues and automating tasks, RMM reduces downtime and increases efficiency.

Will RMM interfere with daily operations?

No, the process is designed to be seamless, with minimal to no impact on your workflow.

Will RMM have access to confidential files?

No, RMM monitors system health and performance without accessing specific file content.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Remote Monitoring and Management (RMM)?

RMM is a way to watch over and manage your computer systems from afar, helping businesses keep everything running smoothly.

How does RMM save money compared to regular IT help?

RMM stops work from stopping, keeps everything up-to-date, and fixes little problems before they get big, saving businesses money.

Is remote monitoring safe?

Our RMM system uses strong security to make sure your information stays safe, even when managed from afar.

Can small businesses use RMM?

Yes, RMM can grow with your business, giving small companies the same kind of IT help big companies have.

Can RMM stop hackers?

While RMM helps a lot with security, it's part of a bigger plan to keep hackers out. It makes it much harder for them but isn't a complete solution on its own.

How often should updates happen?

Updates should happen regularly, ideally right away or at least once a day, to keep everything safe and working well.

What if there's a power cut?

If the power goes out, RMM will start working again once the power is back. Having a backup power source can keep it running during outages.

Can RMM work with my current tools?

Most RMM systems can work with other tools you already use, making everything easier to manage in one place.

General Questions

What is RMM?
RMM stands for Remote Monitoring and Management, a technology that allows IT providers to monitor and manage devices and networks remotely.
How does RMM work?
RMM software uses an agent installed on devices to collect data and enable remote actions like updates and troubleshooting.
Why do businesses need RMM services?
RMM ensures uptime, improves security, and minimizes IT issues by proactively monitoring and managing your technology infrastructure.
Is RMM suitable for small businesses?
Yes, RMM is ideal for small businesses as it provides enterprise-level IT support at a fraction of the cost.
What devices can be monitored with RMM?
RMM supports desktops, laptops, servers, network equipment, and some IoT devices.
